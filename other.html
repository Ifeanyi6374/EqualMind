Other Organisations With Similar Missions

MAYEIN (Mentoring Assistance for Youths and Entrepreneurs Initiative)

MAYEIN is a Nigerian nonprofit focused on empowering youth through mentorship, education, and digital literacy. It runs programs that support students, girls, and young entrepreneurs with life skills, ICT training, civic engagement, and career guidance. The initiative targets underserved communities, aiming to reduce inequality and promote youth-led development. Their mission is to lead strategies that promote inclusive quality education and lifelong learning for all, gender equity, and civic readiness for the attainment of positive youth development.

AEF (African Education Foundation)


The African Education Foundation (AEF) supports access to quality education across Africa by funding infrastructure, resources, and scholarship programs. It partners with local communities, schools, and NGOs to improve learning environments, support teacher development, and empower students—especially in rural and disadvantaged areas. The African Education Foundation (“AEF”) is dedicated to the improvement of health and education in West Africa.
