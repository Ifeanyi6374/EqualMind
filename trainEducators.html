Training Educators

We want to train educators to be more effective in their teaching. We want to provide them with the tools and resources they need to be successful.

Some Platforms For Training Educators

Varkey Foundation – Teacher Training

The Varkey Foundation focuses on improving the status and effectiveness of teachers worldwide. Through its teacher training programs, especially in underserved regions, it equips educators with modern teaching strategies, classroom management skills, and subject expertise. The foundation also promotes teacher leadership and global advocacy for education equity. Learn More

Cambridge Assessment International Education (CAIE)

CAIE offers professional development for educators teaching the Cambridge curriculum. Its training covers subject-specific teaching techniques, assessment strategies, and curriculum planning. Teachers can access online and in-person workshops, certification programs, and continuous support to enhance their effectiveness and alignment with international education standards.Learn More

Teach For All

Teach For All is a global network of independent partner organizations working to expand educational opportunity by recruiting and training leaders to teach in underserved schools. Participants undergo intensive teacher training and are placed in high-need classrooms. The model emphasizes local adaptation, leadership development, and long-term impact in educational systems.
