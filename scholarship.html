Yale University Scholarships USA

The Yale University Scholarship is a fully funded scholarship for international students. This scholarship is offered for undergraduate, master’s and PhD and can vary from a few hundred dollars to over $70,000 per year; the average Yale need-based scholarship is over $50,000.

Fulbright Scholarships USA

The Fulbright Scholarships are fully funded scholarships for international students. These scholarships are only offered for master’s and PhD studies. The Fulbright Foreign Student Program covers a full tuition fee, a living stipend, a full accommodation fee, airfare and health insurance.

Learn More

Simmons University Kotzen Scholarships

The Simmons University Kotzen Scholarships are a fully funded scholarship for international students. This scholarship is offered for Undergraduate studies and covers full tuition, mandatory fees, and room and board.

Learn More

The Lester B. Pearson International Scholarship Program

The Lester B. Pearson International Scholarship Program is a fully funded scholarship for international students. A total of 37 scholarships are available for undergraduate studies. This scholarship will cover tuition, books, incidental fees, and full residence support for four years.

Learn More

Melbourne Research Scholarships, Australia

The Melbourne Research Scholarship (MRS) is a fully funded scholarship for international students. Approximately 600 scholarships are available for master’s and doctoral research studies. This scholarship offers a living allowance of $31,200 per year, Full fee offset, Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC) Single Membership, Relocation grant and many other benefits.
