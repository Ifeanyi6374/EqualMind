Free Resources

We want to provide free resources to people who may not have access to them. We want to provide resources that can help people learn and grow.

Some Platforms For Free Learning

Coursera

Coursera is the global online learning platform that offers anyone, anywhere access to online courses and degrees from world-class universities and companies. Coursera partners with top universities and companies to offer online courses, professional certificates, and degrees. It covers a wide range of subjects—from tech and business to arts and health. Courses often include video lectures, quizzes, and peer-reviewed assignments. Some content is free, but certificates and advanced features usually require payment. Learn More

Udemy

Whether you want to learn a new skill, train your teams, or share what you know with the world, you’re in the right place. As a leader in online learning, we’re here to help you achieve your goals and transform your life. Udemy is a global marketplace for learning, offering thousands of paid and free courses taught by independent instructors. It covers everything from coding and design to business and lifestyle skills. Courses are self-paced with lifetime access upon purchase, but quality can vary since anyone can teach.Learn More.

Khan Academy

They are a nonprofit with the mission to provide a free, world-class education for anyone, anywhere. Khan Academy is a nonprofit platform offering free educational resources primarily for K–12 and early college students. It specializes in subjects like math, science, economics, and SAT prep. Lessons include short instructional videos, interactive exercises, and progress tracking—completely free and ad-free. Learn More

FutureLearn

FutureLearn is a UK-based platform offering short online courses, microcredentials, and degrees in partnership with global universities and institutions. It focuses on career development, humanities, tech, and healthcare. Courses are usually structured in weekly modules, with free access to content for a limited time and paid upgrades for certificates or extended access.Learn More
